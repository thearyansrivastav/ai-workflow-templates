# Cold Outreach Email Sequence

**Status:** Stable  
**Platform:** n8n / Make  
**AI layer:** GPT-4o-mini

---

## Purpose

A structured cold outreach workflow that generates personalized
first-touch emails from prospect data.

Designed for:
- B2B outreach
- small sales teams
- lead generation workflows
- AI-assisted personalization

---

## Workflow Overview

1. Prospect data enters the system
2. Lead data is enriched
3. AI generates personalized email copy
4. Email is reviewed or auto-sent
5. Follow-up sequence is scheduled
6. Responses are logged into CRM

---

## Stack Used

- OpenAI API
- Airtable
- n8n
- Gmail API
- Postmark

---

## Notes

This workflow is designed for targeted outreach, not mass spam campaigns.
